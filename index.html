Dining with the Distinguished

This is a night of banquet style food and networking between ambitious students and business professionals, taking place on Friday, December 5th at Rutgers around 6:00pm. Any student can attend and they will be interested in career advice as well as the opportunity to network with someone as distinguished as yourself. Last year, we had 120 students and 17 professionals in attendance.

Where: Rutgers Livingston

When: December 5, 2014 6PM
